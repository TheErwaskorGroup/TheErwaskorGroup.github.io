About Us

The Erwaskor Group was founded by Nikolay (also known as ErwaskorElektr) and Karsten on December 31, 2023, with one current employee, Cristiano.

We share our passion for technology and innovation through our various projects. Stay tuned for upcoming releases and updates!

Current Projects

Mix OS

Mix OS is a custom ROM for older Samsung devices, based on Android 13, combining elements of One UI and TouchWiz. The pre-alpha version is currently being tested on a Samsung Galaxy A7.

The Pornhwa Reality (Webtoon)

A webtoon inspired by Greifswald, blending Korean webtoon style with German assets. This project is an 18+ action shooter series filled with drama and intense action.

Yandere Commando

A parody of the game *Yandere Simulator*, set in Greifswald and Stralsund. The protagonist is armed not only with melee weapons but also a variety of guns to eliminate her rivals.
