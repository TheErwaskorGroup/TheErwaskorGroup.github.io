About Us

The Erwaskor Group was founded by Nikolay (aka ErwaskorElektr) and Karsten on December 31, 2023, with one current employee, Cristiano.

We are dedicated to developing innovative projects. Stay tuned for exciting updates and upcoming releases!

History

The Erwaskor Group was originally known as *Trev Enterprises*. The first project, **Mix OS** (formerly **Huber OS**), was developed under this name.

Later, Nikolay coined the name "Erwaskor" from a glitch in AI voice output, and the company was rebranded to its current name, **Erwaskor Group**.

Current Projects

Mix OS

Mix OS is a custom ROM for older Samsung devices based on Android 13, combining features of One UI and TouchWiz. Currently, we are testing it on a Samsung Galaxy A7.

The Pornhwa Reality (Webtoon)

A webtoon based on Greifswald, combining Korean webtoon style with German assets. The series is 18+ with intense action and drama.

Yandere Commando

A parody of *Yandere Simulator*, set in Greifswald and Stralsund, where the protagonist is armed with a variety of weapons to defeat her rivals.
